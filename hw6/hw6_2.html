Problem 2

Suppose you are given nnn poker chips stacked in two stacks, where the edges of all chips can be seen. Each chip is one of three colors. A turn consists of choosing a color and removing all chips of that color from the tops of the stacks.

The goal is to minimize the number of turns until the chips are gone. Give an O(n2)O(n^2)O(n2) dynamic programming algorithm to find the best strategy for a given pair of chip piles.
